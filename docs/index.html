First reply

Hi, thanks for messaging [Business Name]. Yes, we do car detailing. To recommend the right package, please send: - car model - service needed - a few photos - preferred day/time Once I have that, I’ll advise the best option.

Price inquiry reply

Hi. Price depends on your car size, condition, and package needed. Send me: - car model - interior / exterior / full detail - a few photos Then I can give you the best option and estimated price.

Follow-up

Hi, just checking if you saw the quote I sent earlier. If you want, I can also recommend the best option based on your budget.

Booking close

If you’re happy to proceed, please send: - full name - car model - preferred date/time - any special issues to note Then I’ll confirm your booking.
