# Final Draft

# The Car Detailing WhatsApp Booking Pack

A practical copy-paste script pack for car detailing businesses that want to turn more WhatsApp inquiries into real bookings.

## Who this is for

This pack is for car detailing businesses that regularly get messages like:
- “How much for detailing?”
- “What package do you have?”
- “Can I come today?”
- “Do you do SUV?”
- “How long does it take?”
- “What’s included?”

It is best for:
- solo car detailers
- home-based car detailing services
- small detailing shops
- mobile detailing businesses

If your leads mostly come through WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or referrals, this pack is built for you.

## What result this pack helps you get

This pack helps you:
- reply faster without typing everything from scratch
- sound more professional and organized
- ask the right questions before quoting
- guide people to the right package
- follow up without sounding desperate
- book more jobs from the inquiries you already get

It does not guarantee every lead will buy.
But it will help you stop losing easy bookings because your replies are slow, vague, or inconsistent.

## How to use this pack in 15 minutes

1. Read through the script categories once.
2. Choose the 5 scripts you will use most often.
3. Replace the placeholders with your business details.
4. Save them as WhatsApp quick replies, notes, or pinned text.
5. Use the daily checklist every morning and evening.

That is enough to get practical value immediately.

## Part 1: 5 simple rules for more bookings

### Rule 1: Do not quote blindly
Ask for the car type, condition, and service needed before giving a number.

### Rule 2: Make the next step obvious
Always end with one clear instruction.

Examples:
- “Send me your car model.”
- “Send me photos of the interior.”
- “Would you like the Basic or Full package?”
- “Do you want to lock in tomorrow’s slot?”

### Rule 3: Keep replies short and easy to scan
Most people are messaging while busy. Make it easy to respond.

### Rule 4: Recommend, do not overwhelm
Do not dump every package at once if a simple recommendation would help.

### Rule 5: Follow up calmly
Many people mean to book but get distracted. One polite follow-up often recovers the sale.

## Part 2: Copy-paste WhatsApp scripts

### A. First reply to a new inquiry

**Script 1: Fast first reply**

Hi, thanks for messaging [Business Name].
Yes, we do car detailing.

To recommend the right package and quote properly, please send:
- car make/model
- sedan / SUV / MPV / pickup
- what service you want
- current condition if relevant
- photos if possible
- preferred date/time

Once I have that, I’ll advise the best option.

**Script 2: Busy but responsive reply**

Hi, thanks for your message. I’m currently with a car now, but I’ll reply properly as soon as I’m free.

To speed things up, please send:
- car model
- service needed
- photos/video if possible
- preferred day/time

That way I can advise faster once I’m available.

**Script 3: Price inquiry opener**

Hi. Price depends on your car size, condition, and package needed.

Send me:
- car model
- interior / exterior / full detailing
- a few photos

Then I can suggest the right package and estimated price.

---

### B. Qualifying the lead before quoting

**Script 4: Interior condition check**

Before I recommend a package, can you tell me the interior condition?
For example:
- light dirt
- normal use
- heavy dirt
- stain removal needed
- pet hair / food spills

If easier, just send photos.

**Script 5: Exterior condition check**

For the exterior, please let me know if you need:
- wash only
- polish
- wax / coating
- water spot removal
- scratch improvement

If you’re not sure, send photos and I’ll guide you.

**Script 6: Time and location check**

Please send:
- your preferred date
- preferred time
- whether this is at our location or mobile service

Then I can check the nearest available slot.

---

### C. Recommending the right package

**Script 7: Basic package recommendation**

Based on what you shared, I’d recommend our **Basic Detail**.

Best for:
- regular maintenance
- light dirt
- freshening up the car

Includes:
- exterior wash
- vacuum
- wipe down
- tyre dressing

Estimated price: [RM amount]
Estimated time: [time]

If you want, I can book a slot for you.

**Script 8: Full detail recommendation**

Based on the condition, I’d recommend our **Full Detail**.

Best for cars that need:
- deep interior cleaning
- stain or odor treatment
- more complete restoration

Includes:
- deep interior clean
- exterior wash
- detailed wipe-down
- additional treatment based on condition

Estimated price: [RM amount]
Estimated time: [time]

If you’d like, I can help you choose the best slot.

**Script 9: Two-option recommendation**

I’d suggest one of these two:

**Option 1: Basic Detail** — [RM price]
Best if you want a good cleanup and refresh.

**Option 2: Full Detail** — [RM price]
Best if the car is dirtier or you want a more complete result.

If you want, I can recommend the better one based on your budget.

---

### D. Sending the quote clearly

**Script 10: Simple quote**

Thanks for the details.

For your [car model], estimated price is **[RM amount]** for **[service/package]**.
Estimated time: **[time]**
Available slot: **[day/time]**

If you want to proceed, I can reserve the slot for you.

**Script 11: Quote with what’s included**

Here’s the recommended package for your car:

**Package:** [package]
**Price:** [RM amount]
**Includes:**
- [item 1]
- [item 2]
- [item 3]

Available slot: [day/time]

Would you like me to lock that in?

---

### E. Follow-up scripts

**Script 12: Same-day follow-up**

Hi, just checking if you saw the quote I sent earlier.
If you want, I can also recommend the best option based on your car condition and budget.

**Script 13: Next-day follow-up**

Hi, just following up on your car detailing inquiry.
Would you like to proceed with the package I recommended, or would you like me to suggest a lower or higher option?

**Script 14: Gentle nudge**

Hi, no pressure — just checking whether you still want to book your car in.
If yes, send me your preferred date/time and I’ll check availability.

---

### F. Handling common objections

**Script 15: “Too expensive”**

Understood.
If you want, I can suggest the most cost-effective option for your car so you only pay for what you actually need.

Would you prefer:
- lowest budget option
- best value option
- more complete result

**Script 16: “I need to think about it”**

No problem at all.
If helpful, I can summarize the recommended package, price, and time needed in one short message for you.

**Script 17: “I’m comparing with others”**

That’s fine.
If it helps, I can explain exactly what’s included in our package so you can compare properly, not just by price.

---

### G. Closing the booking

**Script 18: Soft close for booking**

If you’re happy to proceed, please send:
- full name
- car model
- preferred date/time
- any special issues to note

Then I’ll confirm your booking.

**Script 19: Slot confirmation**

I currently have **[day/time]** available for your car.
If you want that slot, confirm here and I’ll reserve it for you.

**Script 20: Booking confirmation**

Booking confirmed.

Details:
- Name: [name]
- Car: [car model]
- Service: [package]
- Date: [date]
- Time: [time]
- Price: [price]

If anything changes, please let me know as early as possible.

### H. After-service and repeat-customer scripts

**Script 21: Review request**

Thanks again for trusting us with your car.

**Script 22: Repeat-customer reactivation**

Hi, just checking in — if your car is due for another detailing session, we have availability this week.
If you want, I can recommend a maintenance package for your car.

# Self-Critique

## What is weak

1. The product is much stronger now, but it could be even better with package-specific examples for sedan, SUV, and heavily soiled interiors.
2. It would be stronger with a visual quick-start page and a one-page cheat sheet.
3. It still assumes the seller already knows their package pricing and process.
4. It does not yet include an intake form or mini worksheet for customers.
5. There are no before/after sample conversations yet.

## What can be improved tomorrow

1. Add realistic sample conversations from inquiry to booking.
2. Add a one-page quick-start sheet.
3. Add a simple pricing worksheet for different car sizes.
4. Add a customer intake form template.
5. Test more specific titles and positioning angles.

## One permanent rule/template to save for future products

**Permanent rule:** Build the product around one buyer, one painful moment, and one immediate result. Then make the product feel instantly usable by including:
- a quick-start
- copy-paste assets
- a checklist
- a tracker or form
- a follow-up system

If the buyer cannot apply it within 15 to 30 minutes, the product probably needs to be simplified.
