## Copy-Paste WhatsApp Scripts

### 1) First reply

Hi, thanks for messaging [Business Name].
Yes, we do car detailing.

To recommend the right package, please send:
- car model
- service needed
- a few photos
- preferred day/time

Once I have that, I’ll advise the best option.

### 2) Price inquiry reply

Hi. Price depends on your car size, condition, and package needed.

Send me:
- car model
- interior / exterior / full detail
- a few photos

Then I can give you the best option and estimated price.

### 3) Basic package recommendation

Based on what you shared, I’d recommend our **Basic Detail**.

Best for:
- regular maintenance
- light dirt
- quick refresh

Estimated price: [RM amount]
Estimated time: [time]

If you want, I can book a slot for you.

### 4) Full detail recommendation

Based on the condition, I’d recommend our **Full Detail**.

Best for:
- deeper cleaning
- stain or odor issues
- more complete restoration

Estimated price: [RM amount]
Estimated time: [time]

If you want, I can reserve a slot for you.

### 5) Simple quote

Thanks for the details.

For your [car model], the estimated price is **[RM amount]** for **[service/package]**.
Estimated time: **[time]**
Available slot: **[day/time]**

If you’d like to proceed, I can lock it in for you.

### 6) Follow-up

Hi, just checking if you saw the quote I sent earlier.
If you want, I can also recommend the best option based on your budget.

### 7) Gentle nudge

Hi, no pressure — just checking if you still want to book your car in.
If yes, send me your preferred date/time and I’ll check availability.

### 8) “Too expensive” reply

Understood.
If you want, I can suggest the most cost-effective option so you only pay for what you actually need.

Would you prefer:
- lowest budget option
- best value option
- more complete result

### 9) Booking close

If you’re happy to proceed, please send:
- full name
- car model
- preferred date/time
- any special issues to note

Then I’ll confirm your booking.

### 10) Booking confirmation

Booking confirmed.

- Name: [name]
- Car: [car model]
- Service: [package]
- Date: [date]
- Time: [time]
- Price: [price]

If anything changes, please let me know early.
