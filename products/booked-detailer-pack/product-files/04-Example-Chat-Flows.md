# Example Chat Flows

These are simple example conversations showing how the scripts can actually be used.

---

## Flow 1: Price inquiry to booking

**Customer:** Hi, how much for detailing?

**Business:** Hi. Price depends on your car size, condition, and package needed.
Send me:
- car model
- interior / exterior / full detail
- a few photos
Then I can give you the best option and estimated price.

**Customer:** Myvi. Full detail. Here are photos.

**Business:** Thanks for the details.
For your Myvi, I’d recommend our **Full Detail**.
Estimated price: **RM180**
Estimated time: **4 to 5 hours**
Available slot: **Tuesday 10am**
If you’d like to proceed, I can lock it in for you.

**Customer:** Okay can.

**Business:** Great. Please send:
- full name
- preferred date/time
- any special issues to note
Then I’ll confirm your booking.

---

## Flow 2: Customer says too expensive

**Customer:** Wah expensive.

**Business:** Understood.
If you want, I can suggest the most cost-effective option so you only pay for what you actually need.

Would you prefer:
- lowest budget option
- best value option
- more complete result

**Customer:** Best value.

**Business:** In that case, I’d suggest our **Basic Detail Plus**.
It gives a solid overall clean and refresh without going to the full premium package.
Estimated price: **RM120**.
If you want, I can explain the difference between this and the full detail.

---

## Flow 3: Lead goes quiet, then follow-up recovers booking

**Business:** Thanks for the details.
For your HR-V, the estimated price is **RM220** for **Interior + Exterior Detail**.
Estimated time: **5 hours**.
Available slot: **Friday 2pm**.
If you’d like to proceed, I can lock it in for you.

**Customer:** [no reply]

**Business, later:** Hi, just checking if you saw the quote I sent earlier.
If you want, I can also recommend the best option based on your budget.

**Customer:** Sorry just saw. Friday still available?

**Business:** Yes, Friday 2pm is still open at the moment.
If you want to secure it, send me your full name and I’ll confirm the booking for you.
