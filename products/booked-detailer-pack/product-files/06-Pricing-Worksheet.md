## Quote guardrails
Before quoting, check:
- vehicle size
- actual condition
- interior / exterior / full detail
- severe stains or odor
- pet hair
- timeline / urgent booking
- customer budget sensitivity

## Simple quoting rule
If the customer is unsure, recommend **one best-fit option** first.
Do not overload them with every package unless they ask.
