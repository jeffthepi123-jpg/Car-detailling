# Quick Start Guide

## 15-minute setup

### Step 1: Fill in your business details
Use `05-Customization-Worksheet.md` and fill in:
- business name
- package names
- price ranges
- car sizes
- operating hours
- location
- booking details needed

### Step 2: Pick 5 scripts to start with
Start with these first:
1. first reply
2. price inquiry reply
3. package recommendation
4. follow-up
5. booking confirmation

### Step 3: Save them somewhere easy
Good options:
- WhatsApp starred messages
- Notes app
- Google Docs
- text replacement / quick reply tools

### Step 4: Customize lightly
Do not over-edit. Keep the scripts:
- short
- clear
- professional
- easy to scan on a phone

### Step 5: Use the lead tracker daily
Track:
- who messaged
- what car they have
- what service they need
- whether quote was sent
- next follow-up step

## Best practices
- Ask for photos before quoting when possible.
- Give one recommended package instead of too many choices.
- End each message with a clear next step.
- Follow up once the same day and once the next day if needed.
- Keep your tone calm and helpful.

## Simple conversion rule
A weak chat usually ends with information.
A better chat ends with a next step.

Examples of useful next steps:
- "Send me a few photos and I’ll recommend the best option."
- "If you want, I can lock in the 3pm slot for you."
- "Send your preferred date and I’ll confirm availability."
